Creative Nonfiction: The Literary Essay

A specialized subject for Humanities and Social
Sciences strand during the second semester in 12th Grade.

Introduction

This subject mainly focuses on the devices and literary techniques that are being used in writing various forms of literatures. It introduces the students in critically reading and carefully writing Creative Nonfiction as a literary genre that we have in our curriculum. The subject also hones the skills of students in different aspects such as critical thinking, reading, critiquing, and creative writing that will serve as their pillars in brodening their imagine while exploring many literary forms.

Reflect

Given the image from our teacher, we were task to contemplate as we look on it and write our own reflection that tackles the issue within. We should also relate our life in reality to that picture and state valuable lessons that we can acquire from it which might bring changes to ourselves.

Invictus

Read the poem. After reading, analyze it by answering the following questions on one whole sheet of paper.

Poem

Write a four-line stanza using this title, "I Am." In this short poem, write about your thoughts about yourself: your character, fears, and virtues. You can also write about your dreams and aspirations.

Reflection

We cannot deny that for most, reading a non-fiction text materials could be dull and tiresome. This is the primary reason why "Creative Non-fiction" subject is immensely vital in our everyday life. Imagine, reading a lenghty news article without proper utilization of literary devices. It would be a complete failure for a writer to pass the message that they want to convey mainly because they overlooked the significance of writing creatively. Indeed, the key to a person's interest will always be through creativity.
