Philippine Politics and Governance

A specialized subject for Humanities and Social
Sciences strand during the second semester in 11th Grade.

Introduction

Using a historical-institutional viewpoint, this subject introduces students to the fundamental ideas and essential components of politics and government. In particular, it aims to explain how key aspects of our nation's political systems, institutions, processes, and relations evolved over time. And throughout the path within, the subject assists the students in fostering their civic competency by enabling them to better understand their personal and societal rights and obligations.
