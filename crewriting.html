Creative Writing

A specialized subject for Humanities and Social
Sciences strand during the first semester in 12th Grade.

Introduction

The goal of the course is to help students improve their reading, writing, and creative abilities. It also introduces them to the basic principles of writing fiction, poetry, and drama and discusses how well-known authors across a range of genres have used these principles. Each lesson will focus on the analysis of writing methods and the workshopping of student drafts for the improvement of their manuscripts. The ability to mix inspiration with revision is taught to students, along with the development of form.
