Community Engagement, Solidarity, and Citizenship

A specialized subject for Humanities and Social
Sciences strand during the first semester in 12th Grade.

Introduction

This subject focuses on using concepts and research techniques from the social sciences to comprehend, explore, and evaluate issues facing modern communal life. It focuses on community-action projects like participation, solidarity, and citizenship that are inspired by the fundamental principles of human rights, social justice, advocacy, and empowerment for all people. It attempts to strengthen students' sense of collective identification and motivation to participate in communal efforts to advance the common good. This also enables students to incorporate applied social sciences into activities centered around community service.

Community Action Plan

This activity is one of the main projects that we had to comply during our second quarter in this subject. Here, we were task to conduct an action plan within the vicinity of our community that will help the people to live in a better and peaceful place.

Community Action Plan

This action plan has various parts that we had to complete. It includes the overview of our proposed project, the detailed process that will happen on every activity, timeline, interviewing the respondents, and the concluded expected outcome.

Comics

After accomplishing the whole paper, we also had to defend it in front of our teacher similar with thesis defense. During the presentation, we briefly discuss every single part of the project that we propose and answer several queries from my teacher and my fellow classmates.

Reflection

Growing up, I never devoted myself to take part on the activities that my community conducted nor help its advocacy that it trying to promote. I thought that there is no point joining on such activities since there are already many people who are doing their best to help. I'm immensely ashamed as I contemplate on this past attitude that I had. Everyone has their own significant role that should be done within their community. We have duties and responsibilities that need to be fulfilled in our community no matter what our status is because we will all benefit from it.
