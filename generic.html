Reflection

How do you choose a course that fits you?
I believe that in choosing a career that best fits for myself, I should always consider my interest and passion as these factors will always be the source of my enthusiasm and dedication in fulfilling the duties and responsibilities that I'll have in the near future.
Which career is best fit for you?
Existing in this prodigious world for more than 17 years, I've come to realize that the career path that best fit for myself is being a front-end developer in the field of technology because designing using high-tech gadgets always poke my creativity which will eventually leads to a more productive result.
